# 📊 Child Mortality Data Visualization (2000–2023)

> Transforming raw health data into clear, decision-ready visuals  
> for development professionals and humanitarian organizations.

---

## 🌍 Why This Project?

Every year, millions of children under five die from preventable causes.  
Yet, the data that could drive better decisions often stays buried in spreadsheets.

This project bridges that gap — turning **public health datasets into accessible,  
compelling visuals** that support evidence-based programming in development contexts.

Built with the needs of agencies like **UNFPA, UNICEF, and WHO** in mind.

---

## 📈 What's Inside?

| Visual | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| 📉 **Line Chart** | Under-five mortality trends (2000–2023) across 5 countries |


---

## 🗺️ Countries Covered

**Madagascar · Kenya · Nigeria · Ethiopia · World average**

---

## 💡 Key Insights

- 🟢 **All countries show significant progress** — mortality rates more than halved since 2000
- 🔴 **Niger and Mali** remain the most critical cases in Sub-Saharan Africa
- 🌐 **Madagascar** reduced its rate by over 50% between 2000 and 2023
- 📌 **The gap between World average and African countries** is narrowing — but slowly
